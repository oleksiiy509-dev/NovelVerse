# Voice Transformation Safety

NovelVerse uses conservative ranges for pitch, formant shift, speed, energy, roughness, breathiness, warmth, brightness, resonance, stability, and emotional range. Pitch shifting alone is not treated as a natural new speaker; formant-aware processing should be used where available and transformations should stay subtle.

The engine rejects extreme settings that may clip, become unintelligible, create excessive speed, or cause distorted pitch. Tests use deterministic mock audio only and do not clone real voices. Copyrighted or unauthorized cloned voices must not be exposed as presets.
